Onmyodo

  • Daoism Handbook

    Kohn, Livia, ed. Daoism Handbook. Leiden: Brill, 2000.

  • The Development of Early Modern Onmyodo

    Makoto, Hayashi, Dylan Luers, and 林淳. "The Development of Early Modern Onmyōdō." Japanese Journal of Religious Studies 40, no. 1 (2013): 151-67. http://www.jstor.org/stable/41955534.

    This article offers a good and in-depth run down of Onmyodo.

  • Introduction to Onmyodo

    Makoto, Hayashi, Matthias Hayek, and 林淳. "Editors' Introduction: Onmyōdō in Japanese History." Japanese Journal of Religious Studies 40, no. 1 (2013): 1-18. http://www.jstor.org/stable/41955528.

    Onmyodo is an ancient religous system in Japan. With such a long history there are many stories and developments! This article offers a quick overview of Onmyodo as it developed from its early stages and on throughout Japanese history.

  • Abe no Seimei

    Shinʾichi, Shigeta, Gaynor Sekimori, and 繁田信一. "A Portrait of Abe No Seimei." Japanese Journal of Religious Studies 40, no. 1 (2013): 77-97. http://www.jstor.org/stable/41955531.

    Abe no Seimei is a famous figure in Onmyodo. He is often cited in stories and films that involve Onmyodo themes and concepts. Read this aricle to find out more of his feats and stories!
