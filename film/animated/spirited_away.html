In the film Spirited Away, the various plethora of yokai at the bathhouse reflect the diversity of powers and magic abilities that yokai are perceived to have. For instance, the two witch sisters have the ability to steal names and have control over others with them. As well, the have the magic power to cast spells to transform others into animals or other forms such as turning the giant baby into a tiny mouse.

There is also the Buddhist concept of karma as seen in the film. Chihiro’s parents were turned into pigs after they gluttonously consumed all the food at the restaurant without asking. This could be seen as negative karma as the parents were turned into pigs for “pigging out” on food.
