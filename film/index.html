Japanese Religion and Monsters in Films

Here you can find some analysis of Japanese monsters and religion in various films. These films are categorized into 4 main sections: animated, horror, black and white, and modern.

In these sections, you can find the different uses and aspects of Japanese culture included in films from Japan. From kids animated movies with cartoonish yokai to vengeful murderous spirits in horror flicks.

Feel free to peruse to find more about some aspects you may have missed on your first run of a film or perhaps these can introduce you to new films that you might want to add to that Netflix binge next weekend.
