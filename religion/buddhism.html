In Japan, there are many religious systems and beliefs. Although some may no longer be active, the ideas still have a hold in Japanese cinema. Some of the commonly seen ones are Shinto, various sects of Buddhism, and Onmyodo.

This section will offer a short dip into each of these belief systems with Shinto and its reverence of purity and nature, Buddhism and the various subdivisions, and Onmyodo with its use of magic and ideas lent from Chinese Daoism to control and fight yokai.
